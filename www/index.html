Mathematical Proofs

Mathematical proofs are one of the most b e a u t i f u l things that humanity has invented (or, as some will argue, discovered). A great proof convinces the reader of the validity of the claim and teaches her somethingfundamental about the nature of the problem domain.

There are a number of different proof strategies. This site focuses on just one.

Proof by Contradiction

There is a very interesting proof technique called proof by contradiction. To do a proof by contradiction, we assume the opposite of what we want to prove and show that it leads to an impossible situation.

Examples

  • There are infinitely many prime numbers.
  • The squre root of 2 is irrational.
